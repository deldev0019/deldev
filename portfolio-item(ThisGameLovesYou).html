This Game Loves You

(Board Game)

Created in tendem with three other classmates: William Lyons(seen in picture on the far right), Nick Maglio, Derek Borowinski, for the final project of one of my classes, "This Game Loves You" is a player vs Game conflict in which a number of players traverse through a board loosely representing the 5 stages of abuse that can only be ended when the players all experience the 5 stages at least once and agree to end the game together.

The game has a few of mini-games that players can partake in if they land on the right square. However, the rules and goals for the mini-games change depending on what square you are on. I many focused on helping to polish the board design and mini-games along with being present to conduct a few of the playtests.
