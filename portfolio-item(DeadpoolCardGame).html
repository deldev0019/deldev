Deadpool Card Game

2-player VS Cardgame

The Deadpool Cardgame was created initially as a physical card game based on the Deadpool videogame that came out in 2013, with the physical version created as a project with XAVIER MARSHALL for our class. Two-years later, I decided to transfer the project into a videogame, comming full circle in a way.

In creating the card game, my role was mostly in the gameplay, helping to come up with the main core ideas and giving the basic foundations, creating rough drafts of the cards for each character to give them their own unique style while my partner helped to give some much needed polish to everything. As for the videogame port, I was responsible for all the coding involved along with updated Card designs that are still based on the original.
