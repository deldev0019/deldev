My Work

Playable Works

"Identity Seeker"

Based on the question, "Who am I?" the game gives you a time limit to play 3 genres of game with random elements, giving you the decision of what to play and how long to play it. I worked with Wenfei Peng and Dennis Chen, on this project and focused on the Game Design and concept along with most of the coding for the main menu, timer, and drawing minigame.

"Bugged"

Based on the theme, "Chaos", what defines a game? My group argued rules, more so rules in the code. So what happens when code that should work one way fails and presents another option? Well the way to progress turns out to be intresting. Working with Brianna Geck, Paul Nho, Nick Paterno, Leith Reardon, Larissa Truesdell, I was one of the lead game designers, and worked on several of the mechanics used throughout the game.

"Game_Select"

Play as a pixal that must travel between several genres of game to different the bugs ruining it and restore the game to its proper state. Along with Brianna Geck, Ryan Hunt, Jon Lafuente, Sebastian Martinez, Carl Maulfair, Wenfei Peng, I worked as one of the lead game designers, helping to come up with the concept of traveling through multiple genres with similar characters to showcase the difference each genre provides. I also worked as the lead programmer for the Enemy AI in the platformer, shooter, and RPG overworld.
